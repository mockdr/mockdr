## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I try mockdr before buying?**
Yes. The BSL grants a 30-day evaluation period for commercial use at no
cost. Clone the repo, run it, and integrate it into your workflow. If you
decide to continue past 30 days, purchase a commercial license.

**Does the license apply per-server or per-developer?**
Per legal entity. A Team license covers up to 10 developers across any
number of servers, CI runners, or environments. Enterprise is unlimited
within one legal entity.

**What happens after the Change Date (4 years)?**
Each release automatically converts to the Apache License 2.0 four years
after its publication date. From that point on, that specific version is
fully open source with no restrictions.

**We're a non-profit / university. Do we need a license?**
No. Registered non-profit organizations and educational institutions may
use mockdr for internal, non-commercial purposes under the BSL at no
cost.

**We're an open-source project. Can we integrate mockdr?**
Yes, as long as your project is distributed under an OSI-approved license
and is not itself a commercial product or service.

**What if we contribute code back?**
Contributions are welcome under a Contributor License Agreement (CLA)
that grants the project the right to distribute your contribution under
both the BSL and the commercial license. This is standard practice for
dual-licensed projects.
